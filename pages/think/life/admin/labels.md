## Printing Address Labels and doing Mail Merges

- Simplest (same label printed multiple times): 
    - New Word document
    - Mailings | Labels
    - Click New document and save it where you want
    - Enter the address in the first cell
    - Click Labels again
    - Click Print
    - Save document
